monopoly multiverse

There are over 1,000 official variations of Monopoly worldwide — and that’s not even counting custom or unofficial editions!

Here's a breakdown:

By region/Country:

  • Dozens of national and city editions, like Monopoly UK, Monopoly Canada, Monopoly Tokyo, Monopoly Lagos, etc.
  • Localized language and currency editions for many countries.

Pop Culture edition:

  • Franchise tie-ins: Star Wars, The Simpsons, Marvel, Game of Thrones, Stranger Things, Fortnite, and hundreds more.
  • These often include themed tokens, custom rules, and redesigned boards.

Specialty and Collector Editions:

  • Millennial Edition
  • Cheaters Edition
  • Monopoly for Sore Losers
  • Monopoly Deal (card game version)
  • Monopoly Mega Edition
  • Electronic Banking and Voice Banking

Kids & Junior Editions:

  • Monopoly Junior
  • My First Monopoly
  • Monopoly Builder
  • Monopoly Animal Crossing

Niche & novelty editions:

  • Monopoly: Longest Game Ever
  • Monopoly Pizza Edition
  • Monopoly Unicorns vs. Llamas
  • Make-Your-Own Monopoly kits

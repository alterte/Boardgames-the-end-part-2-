7 Tips and Tricks for 100% Orange Juice

1. Know Your Character’s Strengths

Each character has unique stats (HP, ATK, DEF, REC) and a Hyper Card (their ultimate ability).

  • Aggressive characters (high ATK) are better suited for combat and stealing stars.
  • Defensive characters can tank hits and hold onto stars longer.
  • Play to your character’s natural strengths, and tailor your deck accordingly.

Example: If you’re playing Suguri (high ATK and Move), use cards that help you close distance and initiate battles.

2. Master the Use of Stars vs. Wins

When you land on a home panel, you must choose between:

  • Stars (collecting a certain number of stars)
  • Wins (defeating enemies)

Don’t just focus on one:

  • Go for stars if you're good at avoiding fights.
  • Go for wins if you're confident in your combat skills.

Switch your strategy based on the game’s situation.

3. Build a Smart Deck

Your deck should complement:

  • Your character
  • Your win condition (stars or wins)
  • The map and opponents

Must-have card types:

  • Battle cards (e.g., "Battlefield", "I'm Really Sorry")
  • Utility cards (e.g., "Dash", "Holy Night")
  • Trap cards (e.g., "Mimic", "Mix Phenomenon")

Keep your Hyper Card in mind – it's a game-changer and often your best weapon.

4. Manage Risk Carefully

This game is RNG-heavy, but smart decisions reduce randomness:

  • Avoid unnecessary fights, especially if you're low on HP or close to your home panel.
  • Always check your RECOVERY stat – lower values mean it takes longer to revive.
  • Plan routes that pass through your home panel just as you hit the required stars or wins.

5. Time Your Hyper Cards Wisely

Your Hyper is unique and often very powerful.

  • Don't use it just because you can.
  • Use it at the perfect moment (e.g., to secure a kill, turn a fight, or grab massive stars).
  • Save it for when it will swing momentum in your favor.

6. Learn Map Patterns

Each map has different layouts and hazards:

  • Warp panels, drop panels, battle panels, etc.
  • Some maps favor aggressive play (short loops and lots of battles)
  • Others are better for star farming (big loops and fewer traps)

Know the layout to plan efficient and safe movement.

7. Adapt to Opponents

Watch what other players are doing:

  • Are they hunting wins or farming stars?
  • Have they drawn their Hyper card?
  • Do they have a full hand? (They may be saving something dangerous.)

You can interrupt their plans:

  • Chase star hoarders
  • Avoid combat-heavy players
  • Use trap cards to mess with their routes

Multiplayer is chaotic, but prediction and adaptation give you a serious edge.

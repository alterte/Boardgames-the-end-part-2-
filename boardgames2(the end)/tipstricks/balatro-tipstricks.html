balatro tips and tricks

  • Understand Poker Hands Well Know all the possible poker hands and their hierarchy. The stronger the hand, the more chips and multipliers you’ll earn.
  • Focus on a Build Early Decide early on whether to go for a flush, full house, straight flush, etc., and build your deck and jokers around that.
  • Don’t Waste Rerolls Rerolling too often can drain your money. Save it for when you really need new cards or jokers that fit your build.
  • Use Discards Strategically You get limited discards each round—use them to cycle toward your desired hand or to avoid breaking a streak.
  • Save Money for Shop Upgrades Plan your spending. Sometimes it’s better to skip a card or joker to save for a powerful Planet card or Spectral pack.
  • Synergize Jokers with Your Playstyle Some jokers give bonuses for flushes, others for pairs, or number of cards played. Make sure they match your hand-building direction.
  • Remove Useless Jokers Use the Tarot card “The Fool” or a card remover to get rid of jokers that no longer help your build.
  • Don’t Overcommit to Gimmicks Jokers like “Hack” or “The Jester” may seem cool, but don’t let them steer you away from building consistent strong hands.
  • Trim Your Deck Smaller decks mean better odds of getting the cards you need. Use card removers to ditch junk cards.
  • Use Planet Cards Effectively These cards upgrade specific poker hands. Focus on upgrading the hands you consistently aim for.
  • Combine Suits and Ranks Hands like flushes and straight flushes benefit from combining both suit and number strategies. Tailor your deck accordingly.
  • Don’t Be Afraid to Break Streaks If you’re stuck trying to force a straight or flush, consider playing a lower hand just to keep your streak alive.
  • Use Tarot Cards Wisely Cards like “The Magician” or “The Lovers” can drastically alter your jokers or deck—plan when to use them.
  • Pay Attention to Tags Tags give passive bonuses like extra rerolls or higher hand value. Pick ones that match your strategy.
  • Scout for Synergies Some jokers + card combos are very powerful. Example: A joker that boosts all red cards + deck filled with hearts and diamonds.

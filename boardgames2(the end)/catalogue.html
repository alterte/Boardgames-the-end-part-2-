CATALOGUE

monopoly

Monopoly is a classic board game where players buy, sell, and trade properties to bankrupt opponents. Players aim to acquire all properties and build houses and hotels to charge high rents. The goal is to be the last player standing after everyone else has gone bankrupt.

chess

Chess is a two-player strategy board game played on a checkered board. Each player controls 16 pieces, including a king, queen, rooks, bishops, knights, and pawns, each with unique moves. The objective is to checkmate the opponent's king, meaning it's under attack and has no legal escape.

checkers

Checkers, also known as draughts, is a strategy board game for two players on an 8x8 checkered board. Players move their disc-shaped pieces diagonally, aiming to capture all of the opponent's pieces by jumping over them. The game ends when one player has no pieces left or cannot make a legal move.

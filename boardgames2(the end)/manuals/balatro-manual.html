balatro manual

Balatro is a poker-inspired roguelike deck‑builder. The goal: form strong poker hands, use Jokers and upgrades to score chips, and defeat successive “Blinds” (small, big, boss). Each run consists of multiple Antes; clear the boss to advance.

Round start:
  • dealt 8 cards initially.
  • You can discard up to 5 cards, with a maximum of 3 total discards per round.
forming hands:
  • You select a 5‑card poker hand (e.g. Pair, Flush, Straight).
  • Chip score: sum of card values × Multiplier based on hand quality.
hand ranks and multiplier

High Card 5×1
One Pair 10×2
Two Pair 20×2
3 of a Kind 30×3
Straight 30×4
Flush 35×4
Full House 40×4
Four of a Kind 60×7
Straight Flush 100×8
Royal Flush (similar top-tier)

Beating the Blind

Accumulate chips across hands within a limited number to meet the target. You may skip Small/Big blinds for bonus tags; Boss must be defeated.

Post‑Round: Economy & Upgrades

Earnings: money from beating blinds, leftover hands/discards, and interest ($1 per $5 held, up to $5).

shop options:

  • Jokers (150+ unique): add flat chips, multipliers, effects.
  • Tarot cards: modify card suits/ranks or add effects.
  • Planet cards: permanently boost specific hand types.
  • Spectral cards, booster packs & rerolls.
Jokers & Strategic Play
  • Jokers are central—150+ unique cards. They can boost chips or multipliers per suit, hand type, or event.
  • Build synergy: pair Jokers with your target hand type (e.g., Flush‑focused chips).
  • Consider Joker effects like scaling (e.g., +3× per suit), economy bonuses, or hand modifiers.
deck composition
  • Start with a standard 52-card deck, with optional starter decks that offer bonuses:
    • Red (extra discard), Blue (extra hand), Yellow (starting $10), Green (income bonus), Black (extra Joker slot), Nebula, Ghost,etc. .
  • Trim deck size to increase consistency—use Tarot or shop options to remove weaker cards.
